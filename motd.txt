Low-level http(s)/smtp bin

A simple socket server on 80/tcp and 443/tls is configured to dump all incoming data to this directory.
A simple mail server on 25/tcp will dump all incoming emails.
Logs are cleared regularly.

WARNING: Your internet-facing IP will be made available.
An anonymous ephemeral file-hosting service is available at 6969/tcp and 6970/tls.
